Frequently Asked Questions

Shipping


-Shipping rates depend on size, quantity of pieces ordered and delivery location.
-If you live in the Bay Area and would like to pick up your order, please notify in the Customer Notes that is available during checkout. Pre-charged shipping costs will then be refunded and pick up date will be arranged.

Do you ship internationally?
Currently, I do not ship internationally, but am working on making this available for the future.

Care Instructions


Are your ceramics dishwasher/oven safe?
All products are carefully made by hand with stoneware clay unless stated otherwise. Items are dishwasher and microwave safe, but hand washing is recommended to ensure that your pieces remain in mint conditions.

Do you take commissions?
Reach and ask! If it is something that could be made within my style or technique, I'd be happy to consider it.

When do you update the shop?
There is no set schedule that is consistent. I mainly create small-batches which often come in waves. I try my best to announce when those small-batches will be available via its.clay.day Instagram page.
