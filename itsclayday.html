Assignment 2: Choose Your Own Adventure Website

A website for a makeshift studio located in Northern California.

Project Description:

Working in teams of 3, you and your group will design (or redesign) a website for a local small business or non-profit. This small business could be a record store you really like that only exists in in your town ( aka not chain store like Walmart or In-and-Out Burger, a local restaurant you frequent, or your favorite vintage store down the street.
As part of the project the group will work with a stakeholder from the business or non-profit. Students will also conduct research to better understand the target demographic and their desires for the layout and design of the website.
